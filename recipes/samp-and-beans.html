Samp and Beans

Samp and beans recipe

This is a popular South African dish mainly found in the provinces of KwaZulu Natal and Eastern Cape

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Beans
  • Samp
  • Soup
  • Salt
  • Cooking Oil
  • Food Colorant

Steps

  1. Pour cold water into a pot
  2. Pour samp and Beans into the same pot
  3. Add a bit of cooking oil into the same pot
  4. Add a pinch of salt
  5. Place the pot on the stove and switch the stove on
  6. Cook for 2 hours
  7. Stir the the food at approx 30 minutes intervals
  8. Reduce the heat on the stove and put the notch on 3
  9. Serve the dish
