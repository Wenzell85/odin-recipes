Mutton Stew

Mutton stew recipe

One of the popular stews in the world. A dish perfect for a cold weather

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Mutton chops
  • Salt
  • Spices
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Flour
  • Mutton soup

Steps

  1. Wash mutton chops thoroughly
  2. Place chops in the pot and put it on the hot stove
  3. Stir the tripe for it to cook evenly
  4. Cut tomato and onion into small pieces and put the mixture in the pot
  5. Add salt and/or spices and stir for ingredients to mix properly
  6. Pour water into the pot
  7. Cut carrots and potatoes and pour into the pot
  8. Make soup paste and pour it into the pot and stir well
  9. Sprinkle flour and mix with other ingredients
  10. Allow the stew to cook for 30 minutes
  11. Serve the dish
