Tripe

Tripe recipe

Popular South African delicacy which is also known as ulusu, murhodu or inqwema

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tripe
  • Salt
  • Spices
  • Tomato
  • Onion

Steps

  1. Wash tripe thoroughly
  2. Place tripe in the pot and put it on the hot stove
  3. Stir the tripe for it to cook evenly
  4. Cut tomato and onion into small pieces and put the mixture in the pot
  5. Add salt and/or spices and stir for ingredients to mix properly
  6. Pour water into the pot
  7. Allow the tripe to cook for 30 minutes
  8. Serve the dish
